Where we win, where we don't, and how to

Context

Who is Inflyte?

  • UK-based promo platform, founded 2014 — the industry default
  • 20,000+ labels and agencies, 125,000+ tastemakers
  • Claims ~50% of biggest-selling labels on Beatport
  • Notable clients: Sony Music, Ministry of Sound, Defected, Ram Records

Key Insight

Inflyte is powerful but rigid.

Their pain points are structural —
and they're the exact areas where Trackdrop is flexible.

Strategy

Six angles for the landing page

  1. Pricing model — pay per email, not per campaign

  4. Every DJ setup — Serato + Rekordbox, not just Pioneer
  5. Modern player — DRM previews, waveforms, format choice
  6. Real-time analytics — live event stream, public feedback links

Angle 4 — Honesty Note

Be transparent about the difference

Inflyte's Rekordbox integration is direct — promos auto-appear in the Rekordbox library with ratings synced. That's genuinely powerful for Pioneer DJs.

Trackdrop doesn't have that. Our angle is different:

  • We serve all DJ software equally via metadata
  • No vendor lock-in — Serato, Rekordbox, Traktor all work
  • Recipients choose their format on every download
  • FLAC support that Inflyte doesn't offer

Angle 5 — Honesty Note

Content protection: different approaches

Inflyte

  • Forensic watermarking on downloads
  • US-patented, inaudible, survives compression
  • Leak checker identifies the source
  • After-the-fact: identifies leaker, doesn't prevent leak

Trackdrop

  • DRM encryption on streaming previews
  • Widevine + FairPlay dual-DRM
  • Streaming-only mode prevents download entirely
  • Preventive for previews, but downloads are not watermarked

Don't position DRM as equivalent to forensic watermarking. They solve different problems.

Credibility

Where Inflyte still leads.

Don't overclaim. Acknowledge these gaps
or leave them out of the comparison.

Honest Gaps

Things Inflyte has that we don't

RADAR monitoring Airplay tracking across radio, Spotify, Apple Music, Beatport, TikTok, Shazam, DJ sets. No Trackdrop equivalent.
Forensic watermarking US-patented leak detection on downloads. Trackdrop DRM only covers previews.
Pre-save integration Spotify/Apple Music pre-saves tied to promo campaigns. Not in Trackdrop.
Native mobile apps iOS/Android with offline listening. Trackdrop is web-only.
Dropbox auto-sync Promos auto-sync to DJ's Dropbox. Not in Trackdrop.
Established trust 12 years in market. Sony, Ministry of Sound, Defected. Hard to match overnight.
Honest Gaps

How to handle these on the page

  • RADAR: Don't mention it. Position Trackdrop as focused on the pre-release window where delivery and feedback matter most
  • Watermarking: Position streaming-only mode as a preventive measure for sensitive content
  • Pre-saves: Acknowledge the gap or omit from comparison entirely
  • Mobile apps: Reframe as "no app install required — works in any browser"
  • Trust: Lead with product advantages, not tenure claims. Let the product speak.
