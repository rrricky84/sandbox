Ideas spanning multiple themes

Several ideas sit at the intersection of multiple problem areas. These are noted above under their primary theme but also address:

  • Track ID log / listening history (Ricky, Jul 2025) — spans Discovery (#3) and Listener Retention (#2). Logs tracks you've heard across shows, with potential links to buy. Confirmed legally permissible by Nico.
  • "Tracklist Included" badge on shows (Elliott, Jul 2025) — spans Discovery (#3) and Premium upsell (#5). Encourages creators to add tracklists and upsells Premium to free users.
  • Upsell Premium on tracklist preview (Chris, Aug 2025) — spans Discovery (#3) and Onboarding/Education (#5). Stronger conversion moment for Premium at the point of value.
  • Community-powered tracklists (Ricky, May 2024) — spans Discovery (#3) and Creator Promotion (#4). Listeners submit track IDs, creators approve, community engagement drives tracklist quality.
  • SoundCloud-style connect with creators in your network (Will, Apr 2024) — spans Discoverability (#1) and Listener Retention (#2).
  • Twitch competitor strategy (Will, Sep-Oct 2024) — spans Discoverability (#1), Promotion (#4), and Playback (#6). Extensive feedback from Francois K, 4TheMusic, and TORU on chat, API, embed, stats, and re-engagement gaps vs Twitch.
  • Pirate Studios audio capture integration (Julian, Feb 2025) — spans Discoverability (#1) and Promotion (#4). Understand how Pirate gets audio for their Capture App; potential Pioneer integration.
  • Thank-you message button for new followers (Jodie, Oct 2024) — spans Promotion (#4) and Listener Retention (#2).
  • Profile UI tidy-up for highlighted content (Chris, Feb 2025) — spans Discoverability (#1) and Education (#5). Highlighted content currently displays messily; simple fix confirmed by engineering.
  • Profile tidy-up (tabs, list titles, play all) (Chris, Feb 2025) — general UX improvements spanning Discoverability (#1) and Education (#5).
  • Merge Shows/Tracks into a single "Uploads" page with filter (Chris, Aug 2025) — UX simplification spanning Discovery (#3) and Education (#5).
  • Lower barrier to in-browser live streaming (Will, Apr 2024) — spans Education (#5) and Playback (#6). Allow mixing audio from another browser tab so you don't need extra gear to stream.
